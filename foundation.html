Advancing strategic philanthropy through meaningful grants and community partnerships. The AL King Family Foundation is dedicated to creating lasting positive impact in health, education, veterans’ services, and environmental conservation across rural and underserved communities.

Our Mission

The AL King Family Foundation commits to strategic philanthropy that addresses critical needs in health and human services, supports our nation’s veterans and communities, and strengthens education and environmental stewardship. Through thoughtful grant making and long-term partnerships, we work to build stronger, more resilient communities where all individuals can thrive.

Our Story

The AL King Family Foundation was established by Al King III, whose formative years in rural Maine shaped a lifelong commitment to community welfare and grassroots impact. Growing up in Maine's remote communities, Al witnessed firsthand the challenges faced by rural populations—limited access to healthcare, educational disparities, and economic pressures that tested family and community resilience.

These early experiences instilled in Al a deep appreciation for the power of strategic giving and community investment. The Foundation carries forward this vision, working with local organizations and partners to address systemic challenges while honoring the values of self-reliance, mutual support, and stewardship that define rural America. Through deliberate philanthropy, the AL King Family Foundation seeks to replicate the kind of transformative support that can change lives and strengthen entire communities.
