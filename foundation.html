Our Mission

The Al King Family Foundation is committed to advancing the quality of life and creating opportunities for communities in need, with particular focus on rural America where the King family's roots run deep.

Our Story

Al King III grew up in rural Maine, where close-knit communities and strong family values shaped his worldview. Those formative years instilled a deep commitment to giving back to the communities and causes that matter most. The Al King Family Foundation represents the realization of that commitment, channeling resources and energy toward meaningful change across multiple sectors.

2025 Landmark Gift

FF Fire & Rescue Ambulance Donation: $367,600

In recognition of the essential services provided by rural emergency responders, the Al King Family Foundation donated $367,600 to FF Fire & Rescue in rural Maine to fund a critical new ambulance. This gift exemplifies our commitment to supporting infrastructure that saves lives in underserved communities.

Foundation Structure

The Al King Family Foundation is organized as a Delaware LLC, combining the governance flexibility of a limited liability company with the philanthropic mission of a family foundation. This structure enables efficient grant-making, strategic planning, and coordinated charitable impact across all focus areas.
