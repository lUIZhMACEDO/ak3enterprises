About This Property

A quintessential c.1884 Rufus Wakeman home nestled in Westport, CT. Set on a beautiful, level, professionally landscaped, and private 0.82-acre lot, this cherished residence seamlessly blends historic character with modern-day comfort. High ceilings, intricate moldings, and wide plank floors showcase the craftsmanship of a bygone era, while oversized windows flood the home with natural light. The inviting entryway features a bespoke oversized front door and the original handcrafted stairway banister. Gracious living and dining rooms are complemented by a cozy den and a sun-filled home office. The kitchen, dining area, and family room seamlessly flow onto the spacious patio and lush backyard, creating the perfect setting for indoor-outdoor living.
